- Runtime logs and artifacts (document_register.jsonl, trade_ledger.jsonl)

- QUICK_START_GUIDE.md, LLM_INSTRUCTIONS.md, LLM_MEMORY.md, MISSION.md

CORE STRATEGY CONTEXT (Read Before Analysis)

The primary edge is structural information asymmetry:

  Foreign companies file material disclosures on home exchanges in local
  languages. US desks don't monitor these feeds. Unsponsored OTC ADRs —
  created without company involvement, no SEC filing, no US IR — lag the
  home market price by minutes to hours.

  Home market CLOSED = better edge (no price anchor, OTC is only instrument).
  All 16 feeds run 09:30–16:00 EST regardless of home market hours.

Signal weighting applies four multipliers against a $5,000 base:
  window_type × adr_type × edge_score × liquidity × time_since_close

New gates introduced this cycle:
  1. Liquidity gate: skip if OTC dollar_volume < MIN_OTC_DOLLAR_VOLUME
  2. Confidence floor: skip if ranker confidence < profile-specific floor
  Both are logged with new outcome codes: liquidity_skip, confidence_floor_skip

The $200 hardcoded sizing bug was fixed. All trades now size $500–$10,000.

---

PRIMARY ANALYSIS OBJECTIVES

Focus this cycle on:

1. **Gate calibration** — are liquidity_skip and confidence_floor_skip rates
   appropriate? Check document_register.jsonl for these codes. If >30% of
   home-exchange signals are hitting these gates, thresholds may be too tight.

2. **Feed reliability** — which of the 16 exchange adapters are failing?
   Check for HTTP errors, parsing failures, rate-limit warnings per feed.

3. **KRX status** — is DART_API_KEY set? How many Korean companies are being
   skipped per session?

4. **Asian signal quality** — are TSE/KRX/HKEX/ASX signals reaching execution?
   If sentry rejection rate is high on Japanese-language filings, consider
   prompt tuning in llm.py.

5. **Priced-in false positives** — Asian filings are often 5–8 hours old when
   US market opens. The freshness_decay and priced_in flags may be blocking
   valid signals. Check for priced_in_flag=True on Asian trades.

ANALYSIS PROCESS

### Step 1 — System Understanding (10 minutes)

From code, confirm:
- All 16 adapters are in FEED_ADAPTER_MAP (not just 5 as in previous version)
- _us_market_open() gate is active in search_watchlist_feeds()
- signal_weighting is called at BOTH sentry threshold adjustment AND sizing
- weight_rationale appears in trade ledger position_sizing block
- company_meta_map flows: watchlist → runner → ScanSettings → application.py

### Step 2 — Log Analysis

Examine document_register.jsonl. Find and count:

  liquidity_skip            — OTC volume too thin
  confidence_floor_skip     — ranker confidence below floor
  sentry1_rejected          — by what threshold? Was adjustment applied?
  already_priced_in         — how often on Asian filings?
  feed=TSE/KRX/ASX etc      — are home-exchange documents reaching this stage?
  DART_API_KEY              — count warning occurrences

Examine trade_ledger.jsonl:
  weight_rationale          — what multipliers were applied?
  target_usd distribution   — median, range, outliers?
  window_type distribution  — are home_closed trades executing?

### Step 3 — Memory Comparison

Check LLM_MEMORY.md against current evidence:
- "window_type=unknown for European feeds" — still present?
- "KRX DART API key" — set up now?
- "OTC volume verification" — done?

### Step 4 — Identify Improvements

Evidence-driven only. Good examples:

  Problem: 40% of TSE signals hit confidence_floor_skip
  Evidence: document_register shows confidence_floor_skip for 8 TSE docs
  Fix: Lower confidence_floor for home_closed_us_open from 55 to 50 in
       signal_weighting.py
  Risk: Low — still requires sentry passage first

  Problem: B3FeedAdapter returns empty for all 10 Brazilian companies
  Evidence: feed=B3 items_found=0 in all session logs
  Fix: Check CVM RSS URL; may need alternate endpoint
  Risk: Low — isolated to B3 adapter

  Problem: BAYRY freshness_mult=0.20 (floor) because published 8hr ago
  Evidence: freshness_multiplier=0.20 in all XETRA trades despite valid news
  Fix: For home_closed_us_open and overlap windows, consider using
       time-since-US-open as freshness reference rather than time-since-publish
  Risk: Medium — changes scoring logic
